Hello,

Welcome to our PONG GAME. 

INSTRUCTIONS
------------ 

For the single player game, you are the top paddle of the game screen. 

Use keys 'a' and 'd' to move the paddle left and right. ( 'a' = left , 'd' = right )

If you allow the ball to pass your paddle 5 times, YOU LOSE. "GAME OVER" WILL BE DISPLAYED ON THE OUTPUT SCREEN
